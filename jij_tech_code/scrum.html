Scrum Master Certification Training

Become a certified Scrum Master and learn Agile project management with hands-on practice, real-world simulations, and certification preparation.

← Back to Courses

Course Overview

This course is designed for aspiring Scrum Masters to learn Agile project management principles, the Scrum framework, and real-world application through simulations and case studies.

Core Topics:

  • Agile Fundamentals & Scrum Framework
  • Roles: Product Owner, Scrum Master, Development Team
  • Events: Sprint Planning, Daily Scrum, Reviews & Retrospectives
  • Artifacts: Product Backlog, Sprint Backlog, Burndown Charts
  • Agile Estimation, Story Points, Velocity
  • Scaled Scrum (Scrum of Scrums), SAFe Overview

Real-World Simulations

Practice Scrum ceremonies and Agile workflows with live simulations.

Expert Mentors

Learn from certified Scrum Masters and Agile coaches.

Certification Prep

Guidance to clear PSM I or CSM certification exams with confidence.

Skills You Gain:

  • Manage Agile teams using Scrum
  • Facilitate Scrum ceremonies effectively
  • Remove team impediments and drive project delivery
  • Prepare for certification exams like PSM I/CSM

Duration:

5 Months (Weekday and Weekend Batches Available)

Prerequisites:

No coding or technical background required. Suitable for freshers, project managers, and QA professionals.

Career Opportunities

  • Scrum Master
  • Agile Project Manager
  • Agile Coach
  • Delivery Manager
  • Product Owner (advanced roles)

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Do I need technical skills?
A: No, Scrum Master roles focus on facilitation and team management.

Q: Is certification included?
A: We prepare you for CSM/PSM certifications but the exam fee is separate.

Q: Will I work on real projects?
A: Yes, we provide case studies and simulations for practical learning.
